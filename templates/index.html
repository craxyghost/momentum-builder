📚 Methodology & Academic Backing

📈 Why Momentum Works

Price momentum is one of the most robust anomalies in finance, documented across 40+ years and 40+ markets. Stocks that have performed well recently tend to continue performing well over 3–12 month horizons.

📅 Why Monthly Data

Monthly data removes daily noise and false signals. Studies show momentum strategies using monthly rebalancing outperform daily or weekly approaches for long-term investors by reducing whipsaws and transaction costs.

⚖️ Indicator Weighting

12-1 Momentum (30%) has the strongest academic evidence. RSI + MACD (20% each) confirm trend direction. 52W High + MA Cross (15% each) add structural context for sustained trends.
