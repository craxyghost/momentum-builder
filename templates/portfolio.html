💰 Portfolio Return Tracker

Validates the momentum strategy by simulating a ₹100 investment in each top-10 NSE stock and a $100 investment in each top-10 NYSE stock at the time of the screener run. Track real returns to confirm these high-momentum stocks actually outperform.

📖 How This Works — Validating Real Momentum

  • When you run the NSE or NYSE screener, the top-10 stocks (score 81-100) are recorded.
  • A virtual portfolio invests ₹100 per NSE stock and $100 per NYSE stock at today's closing price.
  • Each time you visit this page or click Refresh Prices, live prices are fetched and P&L is recalculated.
  • The monthly history chart shows cumulative portfolio return — validating whether momentum stocks outperform the market.
  • Academic backing: Jegadeesh & Titman (1993) showed momentum stocks generate ~1% excess return per month over 3-12 month holding periods.
